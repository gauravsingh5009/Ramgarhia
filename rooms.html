Rooms

Rooms

Ramgarhia Sabha Chandigarh offers 4 well-maintained rooms available for booking, providing a peaceful stay for guests visiting for events, community functions, or personal occasions. Each room is designed to offer comfort and cleanliness, ensuring visitors feel relaxed and welcomed.

The rooms feature essential amenities such as clean bedding, attached bathrooms, proper ventilation, and ample space for rest. Whether you’re attending an event in our hall or visiting from out of town, our rooms offer a homely environment at an affordable price.

The accommodation is located within the Sabha premises, providing easy access to all community facilities, including the Banquet and Conference Halls. With a focus on simplicity, hygiene, and hospitality, our rooms are ideal for short or extended stays, making every guest feel part of the Ramgarhia family.

Events

Charity Events

Ramgarhia Sabha Chandigarh organizes regular charity events to support underprivileged families, provide educational aid, and offer medical assistance. These events reflect our community’s commitment to compassion, humanity, and social responsibility.

In every initiative, Ramgarhia Sabha Chandigarh strives to uplift lives and spread hope. Our charity drives are not just about giving — they are about building a stronger, more compassionate society. By involving youth and volunteers, we encourage a culture of kindness and ensure that help reaches those who need it the most.

Cultural Fest

Our cultural fests celebrate the vibrant heritage of the Ramgarhia community. Through music, dance, and traditional performances, we honor our roots while fostering unity among all generations.

Each cultural fest is a lively reflection of our shared identity and traditions. From energetic performances to exhibitions of art and craft, every moment celebrates togetherness. These gatherings provide a platform for the younger generation to stay connected to their roots while enjoying the spirit of community joy.

Awareness Drives

We conduct awareness campaigns on social and health issues such as cleanliness, education, and environmental conservation. These initiatives inspire members to take collective action for a better society.

Through these awareness programs, we aim to educate and empower individuals to bring real change. Whether it’s promoting cleanliness, encouraging education, or protecting the environment, our efforts unite people toward a common purpose — building a responsible and progressive community for future generations.

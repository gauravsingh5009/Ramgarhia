Our core values

  • Compassion
  • Courage
  • Commitment
  • Leadership
  • Righteousness

Compassion

Our dedication to humanity goes beyond boundaries. We offer compassion regardless of race, religion, faith or nationality, and aim to ease suffering worldwide for all.

Courage

Our global volunteers demonstrate unwavering courage in our humanitarian work. Stepping into challenging situations, our frontline response reflects a strong commitment to making a positive impact, even in the face of adversity.

Commitment

Our dedication to humanitarian values is the driving force behind Ramgarhia Sabha. We consistently and diligently respond to global crises to bring about lasting positive change.

Leadership

As a global humanitarian organisation, Ramgarhia Sabha leads by example. Through innovative approaches, keen collaboration, and efficient resource mobilisation, we strive to inspire others to join our mission.

Righteousness

Guided by Sikh principles of righteousness and justice, we actively tackle the underlying causes of suffering, striving for a world where every person can live with dignity and respect.

Clean water

Approximately 2.2 billion people globally lack access to safe drinking water. Started in 2015, Ramgarhia Sabha's Water4Africa project has been dedicated to eradicating water poverty and empowering villages in Africa to move towards a safer, healthier way of living.
